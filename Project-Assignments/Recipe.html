Perfect Recipe Solution


Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until golden brown and serve with syrup.
Quick Chicken
Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oi
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oi

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over high heat. Cook the chicken slices until browned, about 5–7 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil in a small bowl. Return the chicken to the skillet, pour the sauce over, and stir to combine.
  4. Cook for another 2 minutes and serve with rice or noodles.
10 mins Caprese Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (baguette or sourdough preferred)
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Layer tomato slices, mozzarella, and basil leaves on each slice of toast.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
